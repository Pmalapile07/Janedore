SHIPPING POLICY

Free standard shipping on all orders over R750. Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days within South Africa. Express delivery (1–2 business days) is available at checkout for an additional fee.

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days. You will receive a tracking number once your order has been dispatched.

RETURNS & REFUNDS

Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags attached. Sale items are final sale and cannot be returned.

To initiate a return, email returns@janedore.co.za with your order number. Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to your original payment method.

PRIVACY POLICY

We collect personal information only to process your orders and improve your shopping experience. Your data is never sold to third parties. By shopping with us, you agree to our data collection practices as outlined in our full Privacy Policy.

TERMS & CONDITIONS

By using this website and placing orders, you agree to our terms of service. All prices are listed in South African Rand (ZAR). Janedore reserves the right to cancel orders in cases of pricing errors or stock unavailability.
